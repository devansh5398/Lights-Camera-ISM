About Our LCI

Lights Camera ISM is the filmography club of IIT (ISM) Dhanbad created by a passionate guy, Piyush Chaudhary. It was he who worked with full interest to uplift this club. After a year, other guys joined it too and contributed to the same.

And now also, we are growing, learning and gaining more and more knowledge to filter our skills of filmography.
